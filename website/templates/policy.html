Our Office Policy
Garfield Young, D.D.S.

All Accounts Are Due And Payable At Time Of Service

If a procedure requires multiple appointments, payment is required in full at the first appointment. The patient is fully responsible for all fees charged by our office regardless of insurance coverage.

Our Payment Options

We accept the following forms of payment:

  • Cash
  • Check* (only for established patients)
  • Debit Card
  • Credit Card (MasterCard, Visa, Amex, and Discover)
  • CareCredit Flexible Finance
  • Lending Club Flexible Finance
  • Paypal
  • Venmo

*Checks are not accepted for new patients and emergency patients.

Patients With Insurance

As a courtesy to you, our office will submit claims to your insurance provider. If for any reason the claims go unpaid, you will be responsible for all charges. The patient is responsible for the estimated non-covered portion, procedures and/or deductibles at the time of service.

All Children must be accompanied by their Parents to receive treatment.

Parents accompanying their children are financially responsible for payment and must make prior arrangements for payment (cash or credit card authorization).

Cancellation Policy

Please note that once you have an appointment, we make every effort to reserve that time especially for you. Should you for any reason, find that you must cancel or reschedule your appointment, we ask that you give us at least 48 hours notice in order to avoid a late cancellation fee.

We are devoted to restoring and enhancing the natural beauty of your smile using conservative, state-of-the-art procedures that result in a beautiful, long lasting smile!
