Do You Accept My Dental Insurance?

YES! We Accept All Dental PPO Insurances

Call us at 734-668-1616 to confirm your specific plan. If we don't carry it, we will try to add it.

We Do Not Accept HMO dental plans unfortunately

But, we will submit claims on your behalf to save you time and hassle!

Please Advise

There is no insurance plan that will cover all dental expenses. Most insurance companies pay a percentage of the charge. It is always the patient's responsibility to pay any deductible amount, co-insurance, or any other balance not paid by their insurance company.
